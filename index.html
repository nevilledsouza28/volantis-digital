About Us

Volantis Digital Media LLP is a global advertising technology platform for programmatic media buying and reducing the complexity of the marketing technology ecosystem. Volantis Digital helps advertisers reach out directly to publishers and create customized ad spaces. We are headquartered in Mumbai, India, with an operations office in Goa, India.

  • 2017-2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Volantis Digital Media LLP is founded by Terence Rodrigues and Bhalchandra Gaitonde, who together bring with them over 25 years of digital advertising experience in sales, marketing, monetization and campaign management.

  • March 2018

    An Agency is Born

    We started using a robust and a high quality adserver that allows us to scale volumes.

  • June 2018

    Transition to Full Service

    We now have tie ups with top quality advertisers and exchanges as we give them access to high quality traffic through our custom and incremental units.

We use them in order to track when we deliver an advertising impression so that we can correctly credit it to a Demand Partner and invoice that Demand Partner. We also drop a cookie for counting frequency of exposure to a particular ad to meet a Demand Partner’s campaign objective around frequency capping. As a buyer of all types of inventory available for sale on sell-side platforms and exchanges (Supply Partner), we do map anonymized user identification number (UID) against Supply Partner’s anonymized UID so as to gain handle to the underlying impression and be able to serve appropriate ad on that impression.
